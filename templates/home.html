About Us

iDiet is a long-established health, nutrition and fitness authority that inspires readers to eat smart and live a healthier lifestyle.

As a leader in translating cutting-edge nutrition research into recipes and guidance for the home cook, we offer the tools you need to make better choices for everyday healthy living.

Each recipe is tested by our staff of Test Kitchens professionals and registered dietitians to meet stringent nutritional requirements and high flavour standards; each story is vetted by our advisory board of leading physicians, nutrition experts, and research specialists.
